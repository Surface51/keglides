Recessed Glides

The Original KEGlide fits about 80% of all legs used on commercial kitchen equipment today—equipment simply sit inside the surface hole. When all legs are inserted, equipment can be moved easily for cleaning or servicing. The tiny weep hole on the side allows cleaning liquids to drain, reducing equipment leg corrosion. Available in standard and heavy-duty sizes. If your health department requires NSF certification an NSF Kit is available.

Threaded Glides

Threaded Glides began as a customized solution for unique applications, such as undercounter refrigerated beverage coolers. Now OEMs use KEGlides to replace larger flanged stainless steel feet to prevent flooring damage; to adjust equipment height for undercounter use; and under HVAC systems to maximize space. All male and female KEGlides are NSF certified.
